Who am I?

Hello! My name is Mitchell Jhy T. Del Poso, I'm currently studying BSIT in CDSP. I'm a Typesetter in a scanlation group called AniGlitch Scans I mainly handle Manga and occasionally Manhwas. This is what i do in my spare time to make myself busy and to know more about the things that I liked. To read the manga that I worked on you may tap the title of the manga you want in the Read section, in the credits you can see the name Tako as Typesetter. That's my credit name in the group.

What software do I use?

I use Photoshop CC2019 version it's good enough and much stable than later versions. I also use TypersTool it's a
program that increases the productivity because it let's you do some shortcuts.
